Early Life And Education

  • 1)He was born as Pichai Sundararajan on July 12, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. His father, Regunatha Pichai, worked as an electrical engineer for the British conglomerate GEC and managed a factory that made electrical components. His mother used to be a stenographer before having children. He has one brother.
  • 2)Pichai grew up in a middle-class home and was a bright student. He excelled academically at his school, Padma Seshadri Bala Bhavan, and earned a seat in the Indian Institute of Technology in Kharagpur, one of the most prestigious engineering institutions in India.
  • 3)He did his B.Tech in Metallurgical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur and then won a scholarship to study materials science and semiconductor physics from Stanford University from where he eventually earned his M.S.

Family Background of Sundar Pichai

  • 1)Sundar Pichai's parents names are Regunatha Pichai and Lakshmi Pichai. He graduated from Jawahar Vidyalaya (Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya) and the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
  • 2)On December 19, 2003, he married Anjali Pichai. He has two children, Kiran Pichai and Kavya Pichai. Sundar is an American citizen who lives in America with his family. He formerly worked for Google Inc., Android Inc., and other companies.
  • 3)Sundar Pichai comes from an engineering family. Pichai's father worked as an electrical engineer for the British company General Electric Company (GEC). Sundar Pichai's mother was also a working lady. She worked as a stenographer.

Sundar Pichai's Significant Success

  • 1)Sundar Pichai persuaded Google's co-founders, Sergey Brin and Larry Page, to create Google's browser. Sundar was instrumental in the ultimate launch of Google Chrome in 2008.
  • 2)Chrome eventually surpassed competitors such as Firefox and Internet Explorer to become the world's most popular browser. Sundar Pichai rose to international prominence as a result of Google Chrome's meteoric success.
  • 3)After an 11-year hiatus after joining Google, he was named the company's next CEO on August 10, 2015. Alphabet Inc. was established in 2015 as Google's parent company.
  • 4)Sundar Pichai received 273,328 shares of Alphabet, Google's holding company, in February 2016. Finally, he was named CEO of Alphabet Inc. on December 3, 2019.

Salary of Google CEO Sundar Pichai

  • 1)Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google, is one of the highest-paid CEOs in the world.
  • 2)Much of his fortune stems from his role as CEO of Google, where he has earned hundreds of millions of dollars in stock options and other remuneration.
  • 3)He also has a variety of other assets, such as real estate and investments.
  • 4)When it comes to 2020, Google's annual proxy filing to the US Securities and Exchange Commission reveals that Sundar Pichai's basic compensation is $20 million (about 14.6 crore INR) as he was elevated to Alphabet CEO.
  • 5)Pichai's basic pay in 2019 was previously estimated to be around 6.5 million dollars (about 51.64 crores INR).

Awards and Recognition

  • 1)He began working at Google in 2004, and by August 2007, he had been promoted to Vice President, Product Management.
  • 2)In 2009, he released Chrome OS, an operating system based on the Linux kernel that allows you to accomplish anything online without the need for a hard drive or software installed on your computer.
  • 3)He is also a co-founder of the open source microblogging application Jaiku.
  • 4)Forbes named him one of the most powerful individuals in business in 2017.
  • 5)He also received several awards, including the World Economic Forum's Top 100 Global Thought Leaders Award; The Webby Awards' Digital Visionary Award; Variety's Visionary Award; Tony Robbins' Game Changer Award; Consumer Technology Association's Excellence in Innovation Award; CNBC's Tech Titan Award, and NDTV Profit's Man of the Year in Business Leadership Award.
  • 6)Pichai was awarded the Padma Bhushan by the Government of India in 2022, the country's third-highest civilian honour.
